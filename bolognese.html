Bolognese Sauce(Ragù)

Description

Condiment based on chopped or minced meat

Ingredients

  • Tomato puree 300 g
  • Beef (beef mince, coarse and mixed minced) 300 g
  • Carrots 50 g
  • Celery 50 g
  • Golden onions 50 g
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Red wine 100 g
  • Bacon 150 g
  • Vegetable/meat broth to taste
  • Extra virgin olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Milk 1 cup

Steps

  1. To make Bolognese ragù, first prepare the vegetable/meat broth.
  2. Move on to the bacon: cut it first into slices and then into strips, without being too precise. Then, using a knife or a crescent cutter, chop it well .
  3. Pour a drizzle of oil into an already hot saucepan and add the pork belly.
  4. Grain it well and let it brown.
  5. In the meantime, take care of the vegetables. Peel the carrot and chop it finely . Then clean the celery and chop it to the same size too.
  6. Finally, peel the onion and chop it.
  7. As soon as the bacon is well browned, add the chopped vegetables.
  8. Mix and leave to simmer for 5-6 minutes. Add the minced beef pulp to the sauté.
  9. Mix and raise the heat. Let it brown without haste, the meat should seal well.
  10. Add the red wine and mix again. As soon as the alcohol has evaporated, add the tomato puree.
  11. Stir to incorporate it. Add a couple of ladles of hot vegetable broth.
  12. Cover with the lid, but without closing completely. At this point the ragù should cook for at least 2 hours. Check it every 20 minutes and add more broth as needed. After 2 hours, taste the ragù, season with salt and pepper. Mix and your ragù will be ready.
